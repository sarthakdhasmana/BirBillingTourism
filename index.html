About Bir Billing Tourism

It all started when we saw a lack of services available for an indivisual or a family who seek a Beautiful Destination for spending their vacations in Beautiful mountains of Himachal Pradesh not just Himachal Pradesh but many Beautiful Scenic destinations which offer mesmerizing views with a fun to make a wholesome vacation and to make a wonderful moment to spend with your loved ones. We came up with an idea to make it possible by providing private cars which would take you and your family to the most astonishing destinations in Himachal Pradesh that is Bir Billing

Bir Billing is a Beautiful village in Himachal Pradesh with various options for a person to spend their vacations in. This village offer many activities such as Paragliding, Trekking, Mountain Biking and camping. It is closer to Macleodganj and Dharamshala which are also Beautiful places to visit and they are famous for majestic mountains. It would be wonderful to have an adventure with you.
